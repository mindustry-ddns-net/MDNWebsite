MDN Rules

Discord Specific Rules

  • Follow the specific rules of each channel (No offtopic, in some channels)
  • Do not display content that could cause a Photosensitive epilepsy without warnings.

Mindustry Specific Rules

  • Do not use any clients that will give you an advantage over other players.
  • Do not grief.
  • Do not waste resources or space on purpose. (Spamming builds falls under this)
  • Do not build lag machines.
  • Do not try to crash the servers.
  • Do not build unsafe links, QR codes or unknown mindustry servers public IP addresses.
  • Do not build 18+ imagery/content.
  • Do not build content that could cause a Photosensitive epilepsy without warnings.
  • No excessive self-promotion
  • Don't build un-respectful things (no harassment, no racism, no hate speech, slurs, or personal attacks)

Chat Rules

Includes all methods of communication.
  • Do not share unsafe links, QR codes or unknown mindustry servers public IP addresses.
  • No pornographic discussion.
  • No excessive self-promotion
  • No spamming. (This means don't post the same message multiple times)
  • Be respectful to others (no harassment, no racism, no hate speech, slurs, or personal attacks)
  • Use English only to talk in public chat

Global Rules

Rules that are applied everywhere in MDN
  • Only appropriate usernames.
  • Do not mask your IP address or do anything that will make you avoid punishments.
  • Do not share dangerous links.
  • Don't disclose personal information
  • Don't promote or encourage suicide or self-harm.
  • Don't impersonate other users
  • Do not impersonate other staff.
