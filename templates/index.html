About us

MDN (also known as "DDNS" and "mindustry.ddns.net") is a server network in Mindustry. We have opened modes for survival, sandbox, PvP, attack, and events. Additionally, we develop plugins (primarily for usage in our own servers) that are open-source and able to be used by other servers.

Survival

Fight your way through endless brutal waves of enemy attacks, all as you build your factory to be efficient, powerful, and productive. In the end, your goal is to survive.
