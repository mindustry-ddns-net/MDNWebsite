About us

MDN (also known as "DDNS" and "mindustry.ddns.net") is a server network in Mindustry. We have opened modes for survival, sandbox, PvP, attack, and events. Additionally, we develop plugins (primarily for usage in our own servers) that are open-source and are capable of being used by other servers.
